---
title: Retrieve details about a specific group.
---

```http
GET /group/fetchAllGroups/{instanceName}
```



### Parâmetros

- `getParticipants` (query, opcional) — - required - Indicates whether to retrieve the participants of the group.
- `instanceName` (path, obrigatório) — - required

### Respostas
#### 200
Successful response
